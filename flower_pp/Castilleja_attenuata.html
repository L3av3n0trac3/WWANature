Castilleja attenuata

Common names
Valley Tassels
Narrowleaf Paintbrush
Family
Orobanchaceae
Family common name
Broomrape Family
Blooms
March - May
Habitat
Grasslands, at low to mid elevations.
Inflorescence color
White or pale yellow.
Stature
4-20", upright.
Leaves
Thread-like, upper ones divided into 3 lobes.
Florets
Slender, white or pale yellow, dotted with pale purple or white; beak straight.
