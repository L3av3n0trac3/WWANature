Claytonia cordifolia

Common name
Heartleaf Springbeauty
Family
Montiaceae
Family common name
Pussypaw family
Blooms
April - August
Habitat
Shaded swamps, seeps, and wet meadows
Comparison
Usually without the pink or red pencil lines seen on flowers of C. sibirica, which is found at lower elevations. Also, C. sibirica has stems on its upper leaves, and small, elliptical bracts at the base of each inflorescence.
