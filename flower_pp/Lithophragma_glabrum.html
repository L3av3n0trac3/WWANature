Lithophragma glabrum

Common name
Bulbous Woodland Star
Family
Saxifragaceae
Family common name
Saxifrage family
Blooms
March - July
Habitat
In grasslands, sagebrush plains, ponderosa or Douglas-fir forests, dry gravelly places, at low- to mid-elevations.
Difference from L. glabrum:
Petals so deeply divided into 3-5 parts, that they look like long, thin petals with very sharp points.
