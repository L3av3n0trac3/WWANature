Amsinckia menziesii

Common name
Common Fiddleneck
Family
Boraginaceae
Family common name
Borage family
Blooms
February - May
Habitat
Open ground
Differences compared to A. lycopsoides
Leaves and stems more shiny than bristly. Corolla tube open in a star, about 10 - 15% of flower width. Most abundant in March and early April.
