Castilleja levisecta

Common name
Golden Paintbrush
Family
Orobanchaceae
Family common name
Broomrape Family
Blooms
March - June
Habitat
Low elevation meadows, including Puget Sound. Endangered.
Inflorescence color
Yellow.
Stature
10-14". Stems many, upright, usually unbranched.
Leaves
Oblong, with 5-7 short lobes.
Florets
Bract and calyx lobes pointed, yellow or occasionally orange-yellow. Greenish flowers shorter than bracts.
